## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Easter Egg Not Triggering?

**Konami Code:**
- Make sure page is focused (click anywhere first)
- Type slowly and accurately
- Use arrow keys, not WASD

**Perfect Aim:**
- Look for 🎯 in bottom-right corner
- Click directly on the icon
- Wait for counter to appear

**Matrix Mode:**
- Type in lowercase: `xavenir`
- Don't type in input fields
- Watch for hint indicator

**Earthquake:**
- Mobile: Shake harder and faster
- Desktop: Click 5 times rapidly (< 1 second)
- Try clicking anywhere on the page

**Time Master:**
- Check your system time
- Midnight: 00:00-00:05
- Golden: 20:26-20:27
- Refresh page at the right time
